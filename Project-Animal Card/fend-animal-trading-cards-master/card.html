Lion

The lion is one of the big cats in the genus Panthera and a member of the family Felidae

  • Binomial name: Panthera leo
  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Order: Carnivora
  • Family: Felidae

Lion populations are untenable outside designated reserves and national parks. Although the cause of the decline is not fully understood, habitat loss and conflicts with humans are the greatest causes of concern. Within Africa, the West African lion population is particularly endangered.
